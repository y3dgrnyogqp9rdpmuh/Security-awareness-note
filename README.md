# Security-awareness-note
Concise and descriptive strategies and security measures explaining the importance of protecting one's online integrity.   
Why Security Awreness?
PROTECT CONFIDENTIAL INTEGRITY: These strategies serve to prevent and mitigate unauthorised access/breaches of sensitive data either personal or organisational.
CURB CYBER-THREATS: By educating and instilling knowledgeable skill to user's, the reduction of common cyber threats such as phishing scams are gradually reduced.
PROMOTES A SAFE ONLINE ENVIRONMENT: This helps encourage best behavioral practices in the online world where everyone can feel free and safe.
